# The Research

The claim behind [Portuguese Taught Me Kubernetes](./README.md) is not just a feeling. People have studied it.

## What they found

A team at the University of Washington taught Python to adults who had never written a line of code. Before teaching them, they measured two things separately: how good each person was at learning languages, and how good they were at math.

Language ability predicted who learned to code fastest. It beat math. Math explained almost nothing. Their models accounted for between 50 and 72 percent of the difference in how fast people learned, and language aptitude carried weight in every part of it.

The framing behind the study is the same idea as the post: learning a modern programming language resembles learning a natural language, like French or Portuguese, in adulthood.

## The study

Prat et al., "Relating Natural Language Aptitude to Individual Differences in Learning Programming Languages." *Scientific Reports*, 2020.

https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-020-60661-8
